Supported Use Cases

The IDX platform supports the following use cases:

  • Debt Management - Debt Management allows third-party applications to access consumer financial data such as bank accounts, credit cards, credit unions, etc., through IDX.

  • Accounting - Accounting uses IDX to allow users to connect to their financial institutions and retrieve accounts, financial transactions, bank statements, etc. IDX pulls the data and categorizes it for the user.

  • Account Verification for Money Transfer IDX allows you to verify accounts prior to transfering money.

  • Financial Portfolio - Financial portfolio uses IDX to allow users to pull and aggregate all of their transactions such as stock positions and transactions, money market activity, etc.

  • 3rd party Integrations - Third-party integrations allow the user to download transaction and financial information from providers like Salesforce. Or, for example, a small business owner with an Amazon Business account can download all of their transactions.
